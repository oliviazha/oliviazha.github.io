PROJECT SUMMARY:

As the Editor-in-Chief of my school's Yearbook team, I led the creative direction and participated in all elements of the yearbook design process from start to finish during my senior year. This included incorporating the 2017-2018 theme, standardizing visual styles, and assuring the quality of the photography, writing, and designs in each spread. We finished with 120 pages featuring all ~350 students at TAMS, each of our school clubs, and the major school events.

THEME: Through the Looking Glass

The theme choice was inspired by Alice in Wonderland, and the book is divided into 3 subsections:
1. Reflection
2. Perception
3. Introspection
With this theme, we hoped to inspire our classmates to reflect on the shared moments, contribute unique perceptions to those events, and continue to introspect and improve in the next chapter of their lives.
